# Hiso Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hiso (the "App") handles user information.

## 1. Information We Collect or Process

The App collects or processes the following categories of information.

### 1-1. Information you enter or save in the App
- The Hatena ID string you enter in the App
- The list of article IDs you choose to hide
- App preference data such as the selected tab and feed segment

This information is primarily stored on your device.

### 1-2. Information fetched for reading and display
- Hatena Anonymous Diary article data
- Public feed data such as Hatena Bookmark RSS feeds
- Archive and article-body data used for display

The App fetches this publicly available content and reformats it for a mobile reading experience.

### 1-3. Information related to login
- When you sign in to Hatena Anonymous Diary, the App displays Hatena-related web pages inside an in-app WebView.
- The sign-in flow itself is handled on Hatena web pages.
- The App developer does not directly collect or store your Hatena password or similar authentication credentials.
- However, cookies and web session data required to maintain login state may be stored by the WebView on your device.

### 1-4. Advertising-related information
- The App may display ads using Google AdMob.
- For ad delivery, Google and its partners may process device identifiers and related information.
- Such processing is governed by Google's own policies.

## 2. Purposes of Use

We use the information we collect or process for the following purposes:
- Providing core App features such as latest feeds, archives, and article detail views
- Saving user preferences and hidden-article settings
- Supporting Hatena login state and post/reply flows
- Retaining caches to improve performance and resiliency
- Delivering ads

## 3. Data Storage

- Certain information, such as Hatena ID, hidden article settings, and app preferences, is stored on your device.
- Fetched feed and archive results may also be cached on your device to improve loading speed and support fallback behavior during network failures.
- The App may retrieve article display data through developer-operated servers.
- However, the App does not provide its own user account system for creating or managing user registration data.

## 4. Third-Party Services and External Transfers

To provide functionality, the App uses the following external services or websites:

- Hatena-related services (login, posting, replying, and opening source pages)
- Google AdMob (advertising)

The App also accesses publicly available Hatena-related web content in order to fetch and display articles and feeds.

Data handling by these services is subject to each provider's own privacy policy and related terms.

## 5. Your Choices

You can do the following:
- Choose not to enter a Hatena ID, or change it later
- Log out and clear WebView session data
- Manage the list of hidden articles
- Delete the App

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

- Settings and cached data stored on your device may remain until you delete them or uninstall the App.
- Login session data stored in the WebView is cleared when you use the in-app logout action.
- For data retention by ad providers or other external services, please refer to their respective policies.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not designed specifically for children. If a minor uses the App, guardian consent should be obtained where appropriate.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy due to legal, operational, feature, or third-party service changes. The latest version is indicated by the "Last updated" date.
