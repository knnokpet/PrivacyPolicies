# IntervalTimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how IntervalTimer (the "App") handles user information.

## 1. Information We Process

The App processes the following categories of information.

### 1-1. Information you create in the App
- Workout template settings (such as names, durations, and rounds)
- Session history (such as start/end time, duration, completion/partial status, and counts)
- App settings (such as sound, Live Activity, HealthKit save preference, and language)

### 1-2. HealthKit-related information
- Only if you grant permission, the App saves workout records (`HKWorkout`) to Apple Health (HealthKit).
- Saved records include completed sessions and partial sessions above a minimum duration threshold (sessions under 30 seconds are not saved by app policy).
- You can change HealthKit save behavior in the App settings.

### 1-3. Purchase-related information
- In-app purchases are processed by Apple (App Store / StoreKit).
- The App developer does not collect or store payment card details.

### 1-4. Advertising-related information
- On the free tier, the App may display ads using Google AdMob.
- For ad delivery, Google and its partners may process device identifiers and related data.
- Where required, the App uses Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) for consent collection and privacy options.

## 2. Purposes of Use

We use information for the following purposes:
- Providing core App features (timer, history, weekly review, etc.)
- Saving user preferences and improving usability
- Providing HealthKit save functionality (only when permitted)
- Ad delivery and consent management
- Purchase status checks and restore purchases

## 3. Data Storage

- Core App data (such as settings and history) is primarily stored on your device.
- The App is not designed to continuously transmit and store this core data on developer-operated servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

To provide functionality, the App uses the following external services:

- Apple HealthKit (workout saving)
- Apple App Store / StoreKit (purchase processing and restore)
- Google AdMob (advertising)
- Google User Messaging Platform (ad consent management)

Data handling by these services is subject to each provider's own privacy policy.

## 5. Your Choices

You can choose and change the following:
- Allow/deny HealthKit access (via iOS settings and in-app settings)
- Ad consent choices (where available, via privacy options form)
- In-app settings and app deletion

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

- On-device App data is retained until you delete it.
- If you uninstall the App, app data is generally removed from your device.
- Workouts saved to HealthKit can be managed and deleted by you in the Health app.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not designed specifically for children. If a minor uses the App, guardian consent should be obtained where appropriate.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy due to legal, operational, or feature changes. The latest version is indicated by the "Last updated" date.
